# Facilities management and maintenance contractors

This is strong target, especially for small-to-mid-sized firms.

Examples:

Building maintenance firms
HVAC service firms
Fire-alarm maintenance firms
Lift maintenance firms
Electrical testing firms
Pest control companies
Cleaning/facilities contractors
Property maintenance contractors

Why it fits:

Repetitive documents.
Lots of job sheets, service reports, certificates, photos, emails and invoices.
Clear admin pain.
Often limited internal tech capability.
Less need for complex factory/ERP integration.

Good AI Beaver automations:

Workflow	Automation
Service reports	Convert engineer notes/photos into client-ready reports
Certificates	Track missing/expired certificates
Quote follow-ups	Generate remedial quotes from inspection reports
Asset registers	Extract asset data from reports and spreadsheets
Inbox triage	Classify jobs, complaints, quote requests, urgent issues
Compliance packs	Build recurring client document packs

This market may involve some personal data, especially addresses and tenant/contact details, so basic UK GDPR discipline is still needed.
