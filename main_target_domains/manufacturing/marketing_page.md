# AI Document Automation for UK MTO/ETO Manufacturers

## Custom automation for RFQs, drawings, BOMs, supplier documents, NCRs and handover packs

AI Beaver helps UK make-to-order and engineer-to-order manufacturers reduce manual document work across enquiry, engineering, procurement, production, quality and handover.

We do this through professional services: workflow analysis, custom integrations, AI-assisted document processing, dashboard development and controlled automation using the client’s existing software stack.

AI Beaver is **not** a generic SaaS platform and not a ready-to-go product. Each implementation is configured around the manufacturer’s actual systems, document types, approval rules and operating model.

---

# What AI Beaver implements

AI Beaver builds a document automation and control layer across existing systems such as:

* Microsoft 365, Outlook, Teams, SharePoint and OneDrive
* CRM and enquiry management tools
* CPQ, estimating models and Excel-based calculators
* ERP, MRP and finance systems
* CAD, PDM and PLM platforms
* QMS and EQMS tools
* MES and shop-floor systems
* Supplier portals and shared mailboxes
* BI and reporting environments

The objective is not to replace these systems. The objective is to connect document-heavy workflows between them so that document status, exceptions, approvals and version history are visible and controllable.

---

# The problem we address

In many mid-size MTO and ETO manufacturers, critical documents move through several disconnected systems and informal processes.

Typical examples include:

* RFQs and customer specifications arriving through email
* Estimates prepared in Excel or CPQ tools
* Drawings controlled in PDM but shared through email or folders
* BOMs transferred manually into ERP
* Supplier certificates chased through shared inboxes
* NCRs managed in QMS, ERP, spreadsheets or email threads
* Handover packs assembled manually near the end of a job

The operational risk is not only lost time. It is late detection of document errors, unclear ownership, uncontrolled revisions, repeated re-keying and poor visibility of document readiness across live jobs.

## 3. AI-assisted document processing

AI Beaver uses AI where it creates measurable value in document-heavy workflows.

Typical document processing tasks include:

* Classifying RFQs, supplier quotes, certificates, NCRs and handover documents
* Extracting customer names, order numbers, part numbers, drawing references and revision data
* Reading supplier certificates and checking required fields
* Comparing customer POs against quotes and assumptions
* Identifying missing documents in job, quality or handover packs
* Searching previous jobs, quotes, NCRs, concessions and supplier records
* Summarising document exceptions for review

AI outputs are not treated as uncontrolled source-of-truth records. They are validated, logged and routed for human approval where the decision affects cost, quality, compliance, release status or customer commitment.

# Workflow examples

## RFQ and enquiry processing

### Current pain

RFQs often arrive with drawings, specifications, email trails, tender packs and unclear assumptions. Estimators and engineers spend time identifying the relevant documents, extracting requirements and checking whether similar work has been quoted before.

### AI Beaver implementation

AI Beaver can implement:

* RFQ intake from Outlook, CRM, SharePoint or customer portals
* Automatic document classification
* Extraction of quantities, deadlines, drawing references, standards and customer requirements
* Bid/no-bid checklist preparation
* Search across previous quotes, jobs and assumptions
* Routing to estimating, engineering and commercial approval
* Exception tracking for missing or conflicting information

### Estimated impact

Typical target range after implementation:

* **30–50% reduction in RFQ administration time**
* **20–40% faster quote pack preparation**
* **Lower risk of missed assumptions, exclusions or revision changes**

---

## Contract review and order acceptance

### Current pain

A customer purchase order may not match the quotation. Delivery dates, specifications, payment terms, drawing revisions and commercial assumptions can change between quote and order.

Manual contract review is often slow and dependent on experienced staff comparing several documents by hand.

### AI Beaver implementation

AI Beaver can build automated comparison and approval workflows for:

* Customer PO versus quote
* Ordered items versus quoted items
* Drawing revisions versus latest accepted issue
* Delivery dates versus quoted lead time
* Commercial terms versus standard terms
* Open assumptions, exclusions and technical risks

Exceptions are routed to the correct approver before the order is accepted into the operational workflow.

### Estimated impact

Typical target range:

* **40–70% faster contract review preparation**
* **30–60% fewer missed document discrepancies**
* **Clearer audit trail for order acceptance decisions**

---

## Engineering release and BOM handover

### Current pain

In ETO environments, drawings, BOMs and design changes may continue to evolve after order acceptance. Manual transfer of BOMs and revisions into ERP creates delay and risk, especially when purchasing or production starts before document control is complete.

### AI Beaver implementation

AI Beaver can connect PDM, PLM, ERP and controlled document repositories to support:

* Released drawing and revision monitoring
* eBOM to ERP BOM comparison
* Missing part, supplier or material checks
* Engineering change request and ECO/ECN tracking
* Alerts when released changes affect live jobs
* Approval evidence for design-to-production handover

### Estimated impact

Typical target range:

* **50–85% reduction in BOM and revision re-keying**
* **25–45% faster engineering-to-ERP handover**
* **Reduced production risk from outdated drawings or incomplete job packs**

### Estimated impact

Typical target range:

* **30–55% reduction in supplier document chasing**
* **20–45% fewer goods-in or despatch delays caused by missing paperwork**
* **Improved supplier evidence visibility for quality and purchasing teams**
