# Best early non-UK targets

| Priority | Market                           | Reason                                                                                        |
| -------- | -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1        | Ireland                          | Close to UK, English-speaking, easier references, similar business culture                    |
| 2        | Netherlands / Nordics            | High digital maturity, many engineering-led firms, English often acceptable                   |
| 3        | Canada / Australia / New Zealand | English-speaking, less language friction, but smaller or more distant                         |
| 4        | US                               | Large opportunity, higher budgets, but more competition and support burden                    |
| 5        | Germany / Austria / Switzerland  | Excellent MTO/ETO manufacturing fit, but likely needs German-language capability or a partner |
| 6        | Italy / Poland / Czechia         | Strong manufacturing bases, but local language and partner strategy matter more               |
