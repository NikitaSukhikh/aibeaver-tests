# Specialist construction subcontractors

Segment	            | Why it fits AI Beaver

M&E contractors   	|Tender packs, drawings, specs, RAMS, RFIs, O&M manuals
HVAC contractors    |	Quote requests, site reports, service sheets, certificates
Fire-safety contractors	| Inspection reports, compliance docs, remedial quotes
Glazing / façade contractors |	Drawings, revisions, specifications, project documents
Fit-out contractors	  | Scope changes, supplier quotes, handover packs
Roofing / cladding contractors |	Site photos, surveys, estimates, guarantees
Steel installers      |	Drawings, method statements, fabrication/installation docs


## Good use cases:

Use case |	Automation idea
Tender pack review |	Extract requirements, deadlines, documents requested, exclusions
RFQ triage	| Classify incoming enquiries and detect missing information
Drawing/spec summarisation |	Summarise key obligations from large PDF packs
Supplier quote comparison	| Compare supplier quotes, exclusions, lead times
RAMS generation support	| Draft method-statement templates from job data
RFI tracking	| Extract questions, owners, deadlines and status
O&M / handover packs |	Assemble required documents automatically
Variation tracking	| Detect scope changes from emails and revised docs
