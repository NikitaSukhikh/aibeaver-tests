# Technical distributors and importers

This is a good fit if you want less site/project complexity than construction.

Examples:

Industrial parts distributors
Electrical wholesalers
Lab equipment distributors
Medical-device distributors — avoid initially unless low-risk/non-clinical
Machinery parts suppliers
Building-products distributors
Technical catalog businesses
B2B importers

Why it fits:

Lots of supplier PDFs, catalogues, datasheets, SKUs, quotes and purchase orders.
Product-data cleanup is painful.
They often need document extraction, not full enterprise AI.
Less personal data than recruitment, healthcare, legal or finance.

Good automations:

Workflow	Automation
Product onboarding	Extract product names, specs, SKUs, dimensions, prices
Supplier catalogue parsing	Convert PDF catalogues into structured data
Quote matching	Match customer RFQs to products/suppliers
Compliance documents	Track certificates, declarations, datasheets
Purchase-order checking	Compare PO, invoice, delivery note and supplier quote
Customer enquiry triage	Identify part numbers, alternatives and missing info

This field is document-heavy, commercially practical, and less compliance-heavy than many professional services.
